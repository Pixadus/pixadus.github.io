Running Arch Linux on the System76 Oryx Pro 5

Nov 15, 2019

Not too long ago, I picked myself up a System76 Oryx Pro to replace my HP Omen laptop. The Omen, despite being a powerful computer with some pretty high-grade components, had an HP-customized NVIDIA card on their laptop, something that did not play well with Linux NVIDIA drivers.

The Oryx Pro was designed by System76, a company marketing itself as a designer of high-quality Linux laptops.
